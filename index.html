About Us

About Avocado Indonesia

Founded in 2025 in East Java, Avocado Indonesia started with only 5 hectares of avocado plantations and a simple vision: bringing Indonesian premium avocados to the global market.

Mission: Deliver the healthiest avocados while empowering 200+ local farmers through sustainable agriculture.
Vision: To become Southeast Asia's most trusted premium avocado supplier by 2030.

Today, we manage over 500+ hectares of fertile volcanic farmland with modern post-harvest facilities, strict quality control, and export-grade packaging systems.

Sustainability Commitment

We implement zero-waste farming practices, drip irrigation systems, and solar-powered warehouse operations to reduce environmental impact while maintaining premium-quality produce.
